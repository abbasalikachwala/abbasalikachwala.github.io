Quick method

  1. Make TZ with the “TZ flour + milk” above; cool 2–3 min.
  2. Bowl: flour (bowl) + salt + yoghurt + milk (bowl) + oil + TZ. Mix ~7–8 min (low).
  3. Divide and roll; dust as you like; clap/brush off excess before the tava.
Protein labels: flour 4 g / 30 g; yoghurt 10 g / 100 g; trim milk 3.8 g / 100 ml. Calories: flour 100 kcal / 30 g; yoghurt 143 kcal / 180 g; milk 164 kJ / 100 ml ≈ 39.2 kcal / 100 ml; oil 9 kcal / g.
